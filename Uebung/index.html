Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Anforderung von Google

  • Don't use auto generated content
  • Don't do follow-for-follow
  • Don't create a website with no or little own content
  • Don't cloak
  • Don't hide your texts or links
  • Don't overuse keywords (Keyword-Stuffing)
  • Don't send automated requests to Google

Google Snippet

Google snippets contain:

  1. URL of the Website
  2. Titel (important part)
  3. Description

Titel

  • Should contain less than 65 characters
  • Each titel should be unique
  • Use keywords that often get searched

Description

  • Main keyword should be in it
  • Should be between 100 and 145 characters long
  • Unique Description per URL
  • Don't lie in it

Google Rich Snippet

They are special snippets for:

  • Rating
  • Recipe
  • Products
  • Events
  • Videos and Music
  • Persons, autors or companies

Domainname

  • Easy and concise
  • No number, hyphen or umlauts
  • There is no advantages for using Keywords
  • Before register check if the name isn't trademarked!

Content

  • Texts
  • Pictures
  • Videos
  • Audio-File
  • Products

Convincing und useful content are the biggest factors for SEO and Visitors on your website

Searchintention from User's

  • Information: search for information
  • Navigation: to a specific website or corperation
  • Transaction: user wants to buy, download or consume something
